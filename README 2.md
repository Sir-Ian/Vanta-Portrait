# Vanta Portrait

A macOS application for capturing high-quality portrait photos with intelligent guidance and quality assessment.

## Features

- **Real-time Camera Preview** - Live view from your Mac's camera
- **Intelligent Guidance** - Real-time feedback to help you capture the perfect shot
- **Strict Mode** - Enhanced quality control for professional results
- **Countdown Timer** - Automatic capture countdown
- **Debug Panel** - Developer tools for monitoring stability and guidance state
- **Quick Save** - One-click save to Pictures folder

## Requirements

- macOS 13.0 or later
- Built-in or external camera
- Camera permissions

## Usage

1. Launch the app
2. Allow camera access when prompted
3. Follow the on-screen guidance messages
4. Toggle **Strict Mode** for more rigorous quality checks
5. Press **Space** or click **Capture** to take a photo
6. Review your photo and **Save** or **Retake**

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Space` - Capture photo

## Privacy

This app requires camera access to function. Camera usage description is configured in Info.plist. All photos are processed locally on your device.
